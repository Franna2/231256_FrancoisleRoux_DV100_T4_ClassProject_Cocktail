Margarita

Alcoholic Cocktail glass

Rub the rim of the glass with the lime slice to make the salt stick to it. Take care to moisten only the outer rim and sprinkle the salt on it. The salt should present to the lips of the imbiber and never mix into the cocktail. Shake the other ingredients with ice, then carefully pour into the glass.

Ingredients
  • Tequila
  • Triple sec
  • Lime juice
  • Salt

Old Fashioned

Alcoholic (30-40%) Rocks glass

The Old Fashioned is a classic and simple cocktail known for its bold flavor. It's made by muddling a sugar cube with bitters, adding whiskey, and finishing with a twist of orange peel and a cherry. It's a timeless favorite for whiskey enthusiasts.

Ingredients
  • 60 ml Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 Suger Cube
  • 2 Dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel and cherry for garnish

Mojito

Alcoholic: Moderate Highball glass

The Mojito is a refreshing and minty cocktail originating from Cuba. It's made by muddling mint and sugar with lime juice, adding rum, and topping it off with soda water. It's a perfect choice for a hot summer day.

Ingredients
  • 60 ml White Rum
  • 30 ml fresh lime juice
  • 2 taespoons sugar
  • Soda water
  • Line wedge and mint sprig for garnish

Cosmopolitan(Cosmo)

Alcoholic(based on Vodka type) Martini glass

The Cosmopolitan, often referred to as a "Cosmo," is a stylish and fruity cocktail that gained popularity in the 1990s. It combines vodka with triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice, resulting in a sweet-tart flavor with a vibrant pink color. It's a favorite among cocktail enthusiasts and is known for its chic presentation.

Ingredients
  • 45 ml vodka
  • 30 ml triple sec(orange liquor)
  • 15ml fresh lime juice
  • Orange twist or lime wheel for garnish
